whats happening?

basically this is a funky little site where I talk about different fandom interpretations of the sp kids!

let me mention that none of these will ever be accurate or representative enough of both how many different types of interpretations there are of the canon, and the canon itself.

canon is confusing enough as it is, so i can't really say what exactly could be considered a "canon" version of the character. either way it would be considered wrong and right, just like all the other fan's interpretations of the characters.

please don't be like me and get confused and overwhelmed by everyone's different views and opinions of the characters along with the creator's possible intentions. just have fun with your version of all the characters! you'll never be able to get it 100% canon anyways since canon is a mix of opinions (that are also partially decided by the fans) and are too complex to both decipher and follow 100% correctly. like i said, just have fun!
